Espérance de Tunis

It is the most successful Tunisian club nationally Espérance Sportive de Tunis (EST), founded in 1919, is one of Africa's most decorated clubs. With its famous red and gold jersey, EST dominates Tunisian and African football, winning multiple CAF Champions Leagues and local titles. Its iconic stadium, Hammadi Agrebi in Radès, is a fortress where the passion of the 'Blood and Gold' fans thrives. Fierce rivalry with Club Africain, legendary players like Tarek Dhiab, and an attacking style make EST a national pride. More than a club—it's an institution!
